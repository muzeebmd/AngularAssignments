Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is one of the most successful opening batsmen in modern-day cricket and is known for his aggressive style of play. He holds the record for the highest score in a One Day International (ODI) match, with an innings of 264 runs.

He has been an essential part of the Indian team and has contributed significantly in various formats of the game. He has also captained the Mumbai Indians (MI) franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL), leading them to multiple titles.

Apart from his batting skills, Rohit is admired for his calm demeanor and his leadership qualities. His consistency and ability to play in pressure situations have made him one of India's cricketing icons.
